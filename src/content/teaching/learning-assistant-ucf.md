## Responsibilities

Learning Assistant for College Physics II (PHYC 2054). Facilitated small-group problem solving and peer instruction sessions, helping students connect lecture concepts to practical problems. The course covered:

**Electricity and Magnetism** — Electric forces and fields, electric potential, capacitance, DC circuits with resistive and capacitive elements, magnetic forces, and electromagnetic induction.

**Waves and Optics** — Electromagnetic waves, geometric optics (reflection, refraction, mirrors, lenses), and wave optics (interference, diffraction).

**Modern Physics** — Special relativity, wave-particle duality, atomic structure, and nuclear physics.
