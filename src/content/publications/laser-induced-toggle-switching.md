## Summary

We report laser-driven topological toggle switching in Fe3GaTe2 at room temperature and analyze the switching pathway.

## My contribution

I simulated the thermodynamics of topological switching and demonstrated how thermal fluctuations and external fields enable skyrmion formation and deformation consistent with all-optical switching.
