## My Contribution

Designed computational programs using Mathematica to analyze symmetry patterns in different modes of wave functions for high-symmetry, two-dimensional microstrip antennas. Identified C₃ᵥ wave function symmetries in equilateral triangular boundary systems and implemented 1D/2D representations accordingly. Verified solutions for circular boundary problems and validated coefficients calculated by other group members, crucial for the correctness of the plots.
