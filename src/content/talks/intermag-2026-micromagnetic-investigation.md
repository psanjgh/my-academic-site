## Abstract

Micromagnetic simulations of Fe3GeTe2 with thickness steps reproduce LTEM observations and show that thicker regions stabilize dense skyrmion lattices that evolve into faceted patch domains. Energy density maps identify a strong demagnetization-field gradient at step edges that pins domains and shapes reversal pathways.
